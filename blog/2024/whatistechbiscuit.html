Introduction: What is TechBiscuit

It's the amalgamation of websites that came before it, each one I spent months building from scratch into something that resembled a project coming together. Each was meant to be something entirely new, different from the last. Each project fell apart due to either my excitement of building another project or the lack thereof in the current. I kept the code in its entirety knowing that although I had only spare parts, they would eventually be the parts I needed to build my great Frankenstein's monster.

The TechBiscuit is my void of the internet, my blank canvas, my own great creation. It perhaps doesn't look so extravagant, but it's something, and it does what it needs too. If you go exploring, which I implore you too, you'd find it most interesting.
