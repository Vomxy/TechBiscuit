About Me: A Short Introduction

First of all my name is Zack. I'm a 20-year-old cyber security enthusiast who created this website to build an identity online. I have 4+ years of web development experience and have had a couple of websites throughout the years until I eventually built this.


I am learning technology security and forensics one step at a time, and thought building a website would provide the environment for me to publish my thought process and the results of my own research, ideally helping others make the same decisions as me.

My Favorite Books

    Dr Petersons practical and life-changing 12 rules for changing your perspective in a way that builds resilience. It's a 10/10 recommended book to anyone willing to read it.

  • Extreme Privacy: What It Takes to Disappear by Michael Bazzell

    A fantastic must-read for high profile individuals, and a recomendation to anyone trying to keep their private life private, each book is as interesting as his last and is all you need to read to learn about privacy to the extreme.

  • Digital Forensic Diaries by Mike Sheward
